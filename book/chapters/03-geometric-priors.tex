\section{Invariant and equivariant functions}
\begin{definition}[Equivariance]
A map $f \colon X \to Y$ is \emph{equivariant} with respect to a group
$\group$ acting on $X$ and $Y$ if $f(g \cdot x) = g \cdot f(x)$ for all
$g \in \group$ and $x \in X$.
\end{definition}
