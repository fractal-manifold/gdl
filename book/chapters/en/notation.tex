\chapter*{Notation}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Notation}
\markboth{NOTATION}{}

Throughout this book we use the following mathematical notation.
Since the book spans multiple areas (functional analysis, algebra,
topology, differential geometry, and deep learning), some symbols
admit several meanings depending on the context (for example, $G$ may denote
a group or a graph, $K$ a convolutional kernel or a key matrix).
In general, the surrounding mathematical context determines the
correct interpretation of each symbol.
