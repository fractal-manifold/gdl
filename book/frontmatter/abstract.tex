\chapter*{Abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Abstract}

This book develops the principles of \emph{geometric deep learning}: a
unifying framework that derives neural network architectures from the
symmetries and geometric structure of the data they operate on. Starting
from the notions of invariance and equivariance, we build up the theory
through the ``5 Gs'' — grids, groups, graphs, geodesics, and gauges — and
connect it to practical models such as convolutional networks, group
equivariant networks, graph neural networks, and beyond.
