## Invariant and equivariant functions

A map $f \colon X \to Y$ is **equivariant** with respect to a group
$\mathfrak{G}$ acting on $X$ and $Y$ if $f(g \cdot x) = g \cdot f(x)$ for all
$g \in \mathfrak{G}$ and $x \in X$.
