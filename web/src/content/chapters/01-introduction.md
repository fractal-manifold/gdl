## What is geometric deep learning?

Geometric deep learning is a program for deriving neural network
architectures from the **symmetries** and **geometric structure** of data.

## Symmetry as a design principle

A function $f$ is *equivariant* to a group $\mathfrak{G}$ acting on its input
and output spaces when

$$
f(g \cdot x) = g \cdot f(x) \qquad \forall\, g \in \mathfrak{G}.
$$

## The 5 Gs

- **Grids** — convolutional networks
- **Groups** — group-equivariant networks
- **Graphs** — graph neural networks
- **Geodesics** — learning on manifolds
- **Gauges** — gauge-equivariant networks
